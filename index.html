Roulette Search

Take a spin and let Foodie surprise you with a restaurant suggestion that matches your preferences.

The Roulette feature turns the mundane task of picking a place to eat into a game. Customize your search by distance and filters, and let the wheel decide your next dining destination.

Card Swipe

Swipe through curated options and save your favorites effortlessly.

Designed to be as fun as it is functional, the Card Swipe feature helps you find the perfect spot for any occasion. Swipe right to save a potential winner or left to move on—it's that simple.

Map Search

See nearby restaurant locations and plan your next adventure with ease.

Visualize all your options on an interactive map, filter by distance, and tap on locations to learn more. It’s the simplest way to plan a perfect outing.
